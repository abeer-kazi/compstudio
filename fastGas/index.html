a faster way to refuel.

Something as simple as refueling a car can take an individual several minutes to accomplish. This solution utilizes a car’s Apple CarPlay to setup and pay for gas automatically, effectively reducing time spent at the pump.


gas price comparison

Users can browse and compare nearby gas pumps and their prices.

wayfinding

Easy to use, in-app navigation allows for a seamless experience.

geo-location

Through the use of geo-fencing, users are welcomed and guided to an unoccupied pump, allowing for a faster visit.

FAQs


Do I need to install something in my car?
No, you just need to have the app on your phone and a CarPlay qualified car infotainment system

Is the app free?
Yes, the app is completely free on the AppStore.

Is there an Android version?
Not yet, our team is working on the Android Auto version and will be releasing it soon.

Does this work with all gas pumps?
The app only works with gas pumps that have allowed us to setup for them. The app will show you the gas pumps with fastGas enabled.

Can the app support multiple cars?
Yes, the app allows for use of multiple cars by saving each car as a different preset.
